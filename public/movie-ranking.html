The Best Movies According to Pablo

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Shining

A writer takes a winter job caring for an isolated hotel, where a dark presence begins to unravel his sanity. As isolation deepens, his family becomes trapped in a terrifying descent into madness.

Inception

A skilled thief enters people’s dreams to steal secrets from their subconscious. He’s offered a chance at redemption by attempting the impossible: planting an idea instead of stealing one.

Really cool sci-fi movie.

The Rear Window

A photographer confined to his apartment begins spying on his neighbors out of boredom. What starts as curiosity turns into suspicion when he believes he has witnessed a murder.
